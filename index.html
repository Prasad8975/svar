Trusted Quality Assurance & Inspection Partner

At SVAR Quality Consultant, we take pride in being a trusted quality assurance and inspection partner for government authorities, public sector undertakings, and private contractors across India. Our clients rely on us for independent, transparent, and technically sound quality consultancy services that ensure compliance with statutory standards, project specifications, and best engineering practices.

We primarily work as Project Management Consultants (PMC) and Third-Party Testing & inspection consultants, supporting infrastructure projects such as highways, roads, bridges, culverts, dams, water infrastructure, soil investigations, and all types of construction materials. Our services extend from planning and execution stages to monitoring, testing, certification, and reporting, ensuring consistent quality at every phase of construction.

With a strong understanding of MoRTH, IRC, IS codes and relevant government specifications, SVAR Quality Consultant has built long-term associations with NHAI, MSRDC, MSIDC, PWD, urban development bodies, industrial clients, and reputed private contractors. Our commitment to accuracy, accountability, and engineering excellence enables our clients to deliver safe, durable, and compliant infrastructure projects with confidence.
